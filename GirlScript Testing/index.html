About Us

GirlScript Pune is the child community of the not-for-profit tech organisation GirlScript. It is a not-for-profit venture aimed at growing and supporting women participation in the field of technology. Our aim is to create a platform conducive to the holistic growth of beginners and learners - everyone who wants a headstart in their career in tech!

LetsPy '19 Pune

September 14ᵗʰ & 15ᵗʰ

LetsPy Pune is an initiative by GirlScript. It's a two day bootcamp + hackathon for both people who are new to the language or want to brush up their skills. So hurry up and register because what would be better than a hackathon to test your PySkills!

  • Great speakers & mentors
  • Kit and certificates for attendees
  • Free food for two days!
